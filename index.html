Story

Waterhope is a quiet and idyllic town in the English countryside. Nick Draper is the home-owner, he is a 43 year old ex-Head Teacher at Water Hope High School. In the last few years, Nick has left teaching and have become a property investor for local developments in Waterhope. He was a local figure of much ire from the community.


How to Play

Read the case files on each characters and assess the evidence. There are nine suspects for you to chose from. You are a police detective, investigating an arson attack. At 2:35am a phone call reported a loud explosion. You have 3 guesses available. Each guess will be displayed in the leaderboard, for reference.
