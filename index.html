Story and How to Play

Choose a session username and then read the case files on each characters and assess the evidence. There are nine suspects for you to chose from. You are a police detective, investigating an arson attack. Waterhope is a quiet and idyllic town in the English countryside. At 2:35am a phone call reported a loud explosion. Nick Draper is the home-owner, he is a 43 year old ex-Head Teacher at Water Hope High School. In the last few years, Nick has left teaching and have become a property investor for local developments in Waterhope. He was a local figure of much ire from the community.
