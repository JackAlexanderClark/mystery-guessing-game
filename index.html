Mystery Guessing Game!

Rules

  1. Choose a session username and then read the case files on each characters and assess the evidence.
  2. There are nine suspects for you to chose from.
  3. You are allowed 3 guesses to chose from. Your guesses are counted and when you run out, you lose and must restart the game.

Story

  • You are a police detective, investigating an arson attack.
  • Waterhope is a quiet and idyllic town in the English countryside.
  • At 2:35am a phone call reported a loud explosion.
  • Nick Draper is the home-owner, he is a 43 year old ex-Head Teacher at Water Hope High School.
  • In the last few years, Nick has left teaching and have become a property investor for local developments in Waterhope.
  • He was a local figure of much ire from the community.
