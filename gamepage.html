Bobby Lane Fact Sheet:
  • The School Bully
  • Age: 13
  • Bobby had been recently suspended by Nick Draper from school after a box of matches was found in his bag, which he stole from the Chemistry department.
  • Bobby has been accused by Nick of starting a series of small fires in the school.
  • Bobby is said to have a grudge against Nick for his suspension.

  • The Old Gambling Friend

  • Mark lent Nick a large loan from his gambling wins to help set up Nick's property investment company.
  • Due to the divorce, Nick has encountered difficulty keeping up his word in repaying Mark back for the loan.
  • Avid smoker.
Rosumand Baker Fact Sheet:
  • The Village Vicar
  • Age: 61
  • She helped secure land owned by the Church for Nick in return that he builds community focused projects, such as a new village hall for residents.
  • However, Nick changed the proposals to build luxury flats for City Traders, to act as their second homes in the countryside.
  • She saw this as a betrayal of his word.
  • This act has created a great sense of community resentment.
